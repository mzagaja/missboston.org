Thank you for your interest in the Miss Boston Scholarship Organization. We offer two Miss titles (Miss Boston and Miss Cambridge) and one Teen title (Miss Boston's Teen). Our program is scheduled for February 2nd at the Row Hotel in Assembly Row. Rehearsal information will be communicated at a later date

Contestants will not be considered registered for the competition unless the following application materials are received by the deadline:

  • Contestant contact details and production information sheet
  • Contestant fact sheet
  • Community Service Initiative (“CSI”) statement
  • 2026 Local Delegate contract with proof of eligibility
  • Headshot
  • Talent music (please send as a .mp3 file and also bring a USB/CD with talent music to the pageant, as technological issues can pop up)

If this will be your first Miss America local of the season, please ensure you are registered on the Miss America website. Instructions on how to do this can be found in the "Eligibility Requirements" document in the "Helpful Documents" folder."

Additionally, all contestants, will be required to fundraise $30 for the American Heart Association. Instructions on how to setup a fundraising page can be found here. The $30 must be in your spot fund account prior to 6pm Sunday January 26th.

Contestants should be aware of the following dates:

  • January 18, 2025: Application materials are due to the Miss Boston Scholarship Organization at missbostoncambridge@gmail.com.
  • January 19th, 2025 is pageant workshop at The Alley Theater 133 Center St, Middleborough, MA. To register email missbostoncambridge@gmail.com. $150 fee, space is limited. Contestants who attend the workshop will have until January 22nd, at 11:59pm to re-submit their paperwork with any changes.

More information about wardrobe and production will be forthcoming, but for now, we strongly encourage you to order your fitness apparel sooner rather than later. The Miss America Organization is requiring all contestants to wear items from the Miss America Collection for Rebel Athletic collection for fitness this year. We have included instructions for how to ensure MA gets credit for your order in the “Helpful Documents” file. MA’s code is MAOMAGB. We are so excited to have you join our Miss Boston family!
