Thank you for your interest in the Miss Boston Scholarship Organization. We offer two Miss titles (Miss Boston and Miss Cambridge) and one Teen title (Miss Boston's Teen). Our program is scheduled for Sunday January 25th at the Row Hotel in Assembly Row. Rehearsal information will be communicated at a later date

Contestants will not be considered registered for the competition unless the following application materials are received by the deadline:

  • Contestant contact details and production information sheet
  • Contestant fact sheet
  • Community Service Initiative (“CSI”) statement
  • 2027 Local Delegate contract with proof of eligibility
  • Headshot
  • Talent music (please send as a .mp3 file and also bring a USB/CD with talent music to the pageant, as technological issues can pop up)

If this will be your first Miss America local of the season, please ensure you are registered on the Miss America website. Instructions on how to do this can be found in the "Eligibility Requirements" document in the "Helpful Documents" folder."

Additionally, all contestants, will be required to fundraise $30 for the American Heart Association. Instructions on how to setup a fundraising page can be found here. The $30 must be in your spot fund account prior to 6pm Sunday January 18th.

  • Further deadlines and important dates will be communicated at a later date.
